## 表格

### 简单表格

```
dog | bird | cat
----|------|----
foo | foo  | foo
bar | bar  | bar
baz | baz  | baz
```

效果

dog | bird | cat
----|------|----
foo | foo  | foo
bar | bar  | bar
baz | baz  | baz

### 表格对齐

```
| Tables        | Are           | Cool  |
| ------------- |:-------------:| -----:|
| col 3 is      | right-aligned | $1600 |
| col 2 is      | centered      |   $12 |
| zebra stripes | are neat      |    $1 |
```

效果

| Tables        | Are           | Cool  |
| ------------- |:-------------:| -----:|
| col 3 is      | right-aligned | $1600 |
| col 2 is      | centered      |   $12 |
| zebra stripes | are neat      |    $1 |
